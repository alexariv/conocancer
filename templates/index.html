More About Our Innovative Learning Features

Track Your Progress

We monitor your learning journey by tracking the number of videos you watch and complete. Using Bloom’s Taxonomy — a framework for classifying educational goals — we assess your depth of understanding, helping you move from basic knowledge recall to application, analysis, and evaluation.

Personalized Recommendations

During account setup, you’ll select your preferred language (English or Spanish) and indicate your learning interests. Based on your preferences, we tailor video recommendations to guide your personalized learning path.

Engaging and Interactive Learning

Our videos feature embedded quizzes designed to make learning interactive and engaging. You can respond by typing in English or Spanish — or by speaking through your microphone in either language.
